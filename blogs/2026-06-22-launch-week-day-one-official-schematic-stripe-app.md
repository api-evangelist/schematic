Schematic introduced its official Stripe app, bridging billing and feature entitlements so non-technical teams can manage customer permissions directly within Stripe. It eliminates logic scattered across webhooks, feature flags, and spreadsheets, enabling faster pricing changes and custom plan provisioning without engineering involvement.
